Yuwei Huang 黄俞卫

Brief Biography

I received the B.E. degree from Computer Science Department in Beijing University of Chemical University(BUCT) in 2018. My current research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning, data mining and natural language processing. I am especially interested in multi-view learning, ensemble learning, automatic grading, text mining and transfer learning.

If you have any story about your adventures in mldm or nlp, please feel free to contact me.

Professional Experience

Visiting Student. MLDM Group, ICT, CAS Oct.2015-Jun.2016

Joined the machine learning and data mining group as a participant of Innovation and Practice Training Program for College Students in Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Dr. Fuzhen Zhuang, an associate professor at ICT and I was responsible for the front-end development, overall architecture design, front-end and back-end communication.( skills: java, html, css, javascript, bootstrap)

Intern. MLDM Group, ICT, CAS Aug.2016-Jan.2017

Learned about the algorithms for feature engineering, classification, data mining and ensemble learning, and participated in Alibaba Big Data Contest. We extracted over 80 features and used Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting Machine for ensemble learning. (skills: python, pandas, numpy)

Research Assistant Intern. AI Lab, AICFE, BNU Mar.2017-Feb.2018

Conducted research on automatic grading for subjective questions based on bag-of-words models, neural networks, word2vector, knowledge adaptation, ensemble learning and multi-view learning, advised by Dr. Xi Yang (a researcher on artificial intelligence at AICFE) and Dr. Fuzhen Zhuang. (skills: python, keras, tensorflow)

NLP Algorithm Intern. NLP Group, 17zuoye Apr.2018-Jun.2018

Using machine learning and text mining to build robust intelligent education application systems.

NLP Algorithm Engineer. NLP Group, 17zuoye Jul.2018-Jul.2020

I am here to develop robust intelligent education system based on artificial intelligence algorithms and do some researches in machine learning and text mining.
